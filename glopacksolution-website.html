Our Story

Trusted Packaging Partner Since 2014

PT. Global Packaging Solution (Glopack) was established in Tangerang, Indonesia, with a clear mission: to provide high-quality, cost-effective packaging solutions for businesses of all sizes across the nation. From humble beginnings as a small POF plastic reseller, we have grown into a trusted partner for hundreds of companies across manufacturing, food & beverage, retail, and export industries.

🎯 Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted packaging solution provider in Southeast Asia, delivering quality, innovation, and value to every client we serve.

🚀 Our Mission

We are committed to supplying premium packaging materials and machinery while building lasting partnerships with our clients through exceptional service, technical expertise, and competitive pricing.
